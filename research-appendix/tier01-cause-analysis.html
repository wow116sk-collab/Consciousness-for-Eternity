Tier 0 + Tier 1 — วิเคราะห์สาเหตุ

ไม่ใช่แค่ "ไม่ผ่านบาร์" แต่ อะไรทำให้คนรายงานมัน — แต่ละเคสแยกเป็น สูตรสาเหตุ 8 วัตถุดิบ (พร้อมน้ำหนัก) + ประยุกต์ กฎ 2 ชั้น (แพทเทิร์น①=ปลดล็อกได้ / ความรู้②=โหลดไม่ได้) + เทสต์ที่แยกจริงออกจากธรรมดา (จาก 10 agent ขนาน)

น้ำหนัก: dominant = ตัวขับหลัก · secondary · minor

Tier 0 — จุดพิสูจน์น้อย (เคสเดี่ยว/หยิบมือ)

ESTABLISHEDT0Xenoglossy — speaking an unlearned language

อ้าง: Under hypnosis or trance a person spontaneously converses fluently in a genuine natural language they demonstrably never learned in this life, evidencing a past life (reincarnation), spirit possession, or discarnate influence.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantความจำแต่งเรื่องCryptomnesia: fragments of a language absorbed and consciously forgotten (childhood exposure, relatives, church Latin, foreign TV, a glanced-at phrasebook) resurface without any memory of the source, so the subject and investigator sincerely believe the language was 'never learned.' In the Gretchen/Dolores Jay case Thomason documented prior exposure to German via TV and a German book.
dominantบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงThe 'responsive' illusion is manufactured by over-reading: selective reporting of hits, retrospective matching (a correctly guessed yes/no is counted, misses ignored), base-rate neglect on coincidental words, and confirmation bias in believer-investigators (Edwards' charge of selective reporting). A tiny vocabulary plus a few right answers gets narrated as 'fluent conversation.'
secondaryเห็น/ได้ยินผิดListeners hear meaning where little exists. Thomason found the 'conversation' was largely the subject echoing the questioner's foreign-language question back with different intonation, or emitting one-to-two-word replies; non-expert observers perceive this fragmentary, non-native output as native fluency.
secondaryข้อมูลรั่วทางปกติThe interlocutor asks questions IN the target language, unwittingly feeding the subject the very words needed to echo a plausible reply — an ordinary channel (cued, cold-reading-style exchange) mistaken for spontaneous unlearned speech. Casual life exposure is likewise an ordinary source treated as impossible.
secondaryปัญหาการทดลองSessions are non-blind, run by committed believers (e.g. a minister doing past-life regression on his wife), often unrecorded, with no prospective, independent, native-speaker-blind protocol and no rigorous life-history audit to exclude prior contact. Stevenson's 'responsive xenoglossy' criterion is not a controlled test of language ability.
minorสมองสร้างเองHypnotic/dissociative trance and glossolalia-type automatic speech (associated with altered frontal-lobe activity) generate fluent-sounding, emotionally convincing vocalization that gets mislabeled as a real language when it is actually meaningless or minimal.
minorตีความเกินA real, explicable event — a hypnotic alter producing cryptomnesic foreign fragments — is overlaid with a supernatural interpretation (a specific past-life personality), turning an ordinary psychological phenomenon into a reincarnation claim.
minorคนแกล้งOccasional deliberate hoaxing is possible but is not the general driver; most subjects appear sincere, and sincerity plus a believing investigator is sufficient to produce the reports without conscious deception.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
This is squarely an ability/knowledge-acquisition claim, and language is the textbook Layer-2 target: vocabulary, grammar, and phonology are arbitrary human convention that MUST be learned and cannot be 'downloaded from nature.' Genuine responsive xenoglossy — sustained, generative, grammatical conversation on novel topics in a language with zero prior exposure — would be near-unfakeable Layer-2 evidence, which is exactly why it is the strongest test the paranormal could offer. But Layer-2 has never actually been delivered. Every documented case reduces to Layer-1-scale trivia: minute vocabulary (often far under 200 words), echoed questions with changed intonation, one-to-two-word answers, non-native/spotty pronunciation, and traceable prior casual exposure. No subject has ever produced novel, generative, verified fluency under conditions that exclude ordinary learning. The claim requires Layer-2; Layer-2 has not been demonstrated.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: A prospective, blinded protocol: an independent native speaker who is blind to the case and instructed NOT to feed vocabulary holds a free, generative conversation on novel topics, requiring the subject to produce original grammatical sentences and comprehend unfamiliar input — while the subject's entire life-history of language exposure is independently audited to exclude any casual contact. Real xenoglossy = sustained generative fluency with documented zero exposure; mundane = the 'fluency' collapses into echoing, yes/no, and words traceable to prior exposure.

confidence: high · Thomason, Sarah G. (1996), "Xenoglossy," in Gordon Stein (ed.), The Encyclopedia of the Paranormal (Prometheus Books) — with her linguistic reanalyses of the Stevenson cases; and Stevenson, Ian (1974), Xenoglossy: A Review and Report of a Case (University Press of Virginia), the primary claim source. Skeptical critique: Paul Edwards (1996), Reincarnation: A Critical Examination (Prometheus).
CONTESTEDT0Veridical NDE/OBE (Pam Reynolds, Maria's shoe)

อ้าง: During periods of clinically verified absent or suppressed brain function (deep hypothermic cardiac arrest, flat EEG, or cardiac arrest), consciousness left the body and accurately perceived specific physical details it could not have known through any normal sense, proving that mind operates independently of the brain.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantความจำแต่งเรื่องThe 'veridical' reports are retrospective and elaborated after the fact. Reynolds could only report after recovering, still intubated, giving hours to weave real sensations into a narrative matching her expectations (Chris French/Woerlee). Maria's shoe was documented in detail only after retelling, and Maria vanished and could never be re-interviewed. Details grow and sharpen in the retelling to fit an OBE frame.
dominantสมองสร้างเองThe experience itself is a genuine brain event, not evidence of a departed mind. Reynolds' veridical perceptions occurred while the Midas Rex saw opened her skull under general anesthesia BEFORE the hypothermic standstill, when her brain was active and anesthesia awareness (~1/2000 ops in 1991) plus hypoxia-driven vivid imagery are expected. OBEs map onto disrupted temporoparietal/vestibular processing.
dominantตีความเกินA real, explainable event (a vivid brain experience plus a few genuinely perceived details) is given an added supernatural interpretation ('the soul left the body'). The mundane parts are true; only the metaphysical layer is unsupported.
secondaryข้อมูลรั่วทางปกติOrdinary channels supply all the 'impossible' content: bone-conduction hearing of the real saw sound and OR conversation despite earplugs; prior familiarity with hospital equipment and layout; and, for Maria, a tennis shoe that skeptics found was plainly visible by normal sightlines from inside and outside the building.
secondaryบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงRetrospective matching, confirmation bias, and base-rate neglect. Hits are counted and retold; the shoe's ordinary visibility and the many mundane details are not controlled for. A striking coincidence is read as proof without asking how often such a guess would land.
secondaryเห็น/ได้ยินผิดMisperception of real input: Reynolds heard actual sounds and reconstructed the instrument's appearance (saw as 'electric toothbrush,' motor as 'natural D') from audio plus knowledge, not sight.
secondaryปัญหาการทดลองEvery impressive case is uncontrolled, non-blind, and retrospective. The one paradigm built to test it prospectively, AWARE's hidden visual targets, produced zero hits, showing the effect lives only in uncontrolled anecdote.
minorคนแกล้งNo good evidence of deliberate deception; witnesses appear sincere. Embellishment in retelling is memory, not fraud. Included only for completeness.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
This IS an information-acquisition claim (mind obtaining specific physical facts from beyond ordinary senses). But every 'veridical' item is Layer-1 pattern/perception, exactly the kind of thing ordinary residual channels supply: the shape of a surgical saw (heard, plus prior knowledge), overheard OR talk, hospital layout, a shoe visible by normal sightlines. None is Layer-2, un-learnable knowledge that could not in principle be acquired normally (an unlearned language, an un-encountered theorem, a genuinely un-guessable pre-registered fact). The AWARE study placed hidden images that could ONLY be seen from an out-of-body vantage, precisely to demand Layer-2, and got zero hits across 2,060 arrests. So even the strongest cases deliver Layer-1 only, and the single test engineered to force Layer-2 delivered nothing. Layer-2 has never been delivered for this phenomenon.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: A prospective, pre-registered target placed out of all ordinary sightlines (e.g., an image legible only from the ceiling) accurately reported by an OBE patient with independent verification and an adequate case base, i.e., an AWARE-style hidden-target HIT. That single observation would separate paranormal from mundane. To date the count of such verified hits is zero.

confidence: high · Ebbern, Mulligan & Beyerstein (1996), "Maria's Near-Death Experience: Waiting for the Other Shoe to Drop," Skeptical Inquirer 20(4); Parnia et al. (2014), "AWARE—AWAreness during REsuscitation—a prospective study," Resuscitation 85(12):1799-1805 (hidden visual targets: no verified hits). See also Woerlee (2011), "Could Pam Reynolds Hear?", Journal of Near-Death Studies.
ESTABLISHEDT0Past-life regression under hypnosis (Bridey Murphy)

อ้าง: Under hypnosis Virginia Tighe accessed authentic memories of a genuine prior incarnation as Bridey Murphy, a 19th-century Cork Irishwoman, demonstrating that consciousness survives death and reincarnates.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantความจำแต่งเรื่องTextbook cryptomnesia: forgotten childhood material resurfaced under trance as a first-person 'past life,' unrecognized as memory. A neighbour of Tighe's Chicago girlhood home was Bridie Murphy Corkell (an Irish immigrant), and an Irish aunt had told her tales of Ireland; the trance wove these dormant strands into a confabulated Bridey narrative.
secondaryสมองสร้างเองThe hypnotic/absorbed brain state lowers reality-monitoring and boosts vivid imagery and suggestibility, so an internally generated fantasy is experienced as a lived, remembered episode rather than as invention.
secondaryข้อมูลรั่วทางปกติAll the 'Irish' content had ordinary, in-life sources — the immigrant neighbour, the aunt's stories, Chicago's Irish-American community, popular culture — mistaken for information that could only come from a real past life.
minorปัญหาการทดลองBernstein was an untrained, non-blind, believing hypnotist who led with expectation-laden questions; no controls, no blinding, and the 'past life' was defined as real up front — exactly the conditions Spanos et al. showed shape the content.
minorบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงRetrospective matching and confirmation: vague or unverifiable 'hits' (names, place-feel) were counted as accurate, checkable errors were ignored, and no base-rate for 'lucky' period detail was applied.
minorตีความเกินA real, mundane psychological event (a vivid hypnotic fantasy built from forgotten memories) had a supernatural interpretation — reincarnation, survival of consciousness — bolted onto it.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
This is an information/ability-acquisition claim: Bridey supposedly 'knew' things only a real 19th-century Irishwoman could. Layer 1 (pattern/imagery/emotional feel of 'Irishness') is freely available from ordinary exposure and needs no past life. Genuine evidence would require Layer 2 — un-learnable, un-encountered knowledge: fluent period Irish Gaelic she demonstrably never studied, or a specific, checkable, un-guessable fact (a verifiable birth/marriage/death record, an archival detail) traceable to NO source in her biography. Layer 2 was never delivered: she spoke English (not Irish), the verifiable 'facts' traced back to her Chicago childhood (neighbour Bridie Murphy Corkell, the aunt), and the un-traceable claims (Bridey's dates, family, burial) were never confirmed by any Irish record. Only Layer 1 was ever present.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: Regress a subject under a hypnotist blind to their full biography and have them produce Layer-2 content prospectively verified against independent archives — e.g., fluently converse in a period language they never learned, or state a specific past-life fact (a name/date/place) that is later confirmed by records AND cannot be traced to any ordinary source in the subject's life. Bridey (and every replication since) yields only material reducible to the subject's own biography and culture.

confidence: high · Spanos, Menary, Gabora & DuBreuil (1991), "Secondary identity enactments during hypnotic past-life regression," Journal of Personality and Social Psychology 61(2):308-320 (past-life reports are experimenter-shaped fantasy constructions, historically inaccurate); Chicago American investigation (1956) and Melvin Harris, "Investigating the Unexplained" (1986) — Tighe's Bridey material traced to childhood neighbour Bridie Murphy Corkell (cryptomnesia).
CONTESTEDT0Crisis apparitions (perceiving a distant person at their death)

อ้าง: At the moment of a person's death or grave crisis, a distant loved one receives a veridical impression of it (a vision, voice, or sense of presence) through a non-physical channel — telepathy or a soul signal — carrying knowledge they had no ordinary way of obtaining.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงBase-rate neglect plus selective/retrospective matching. Billions of people intermittently think of, dream of, or 'sense' loved ones every day; deaths occur constantly. Only the rare coincidences are noticed, remembered, and reported (the misses vanish). The SPR's headline '440x chance' rests on contested estimates of the hallucination and death base rates and a post-hoc 12-hour window; tighten the assumptions and the excess shrinks.
dominantความจำแต่งเรื่องThe timing is reconstructed after the death becomes known. People unconsciously slide the remembered time of a vague experience to line up with the death ('retrospective time-matching'), sharpen fuzzy impressions into a specific figure, and firm up detail on retelling. Most census testimony was collected months to years later, so the 'coincidence' is partly built in memory.
secondaryสมองสร้างเองSupplies the raw experience. Bereavement/grief hallucinations are common (roughly a third or more of the bereaved), and hypnagogic/hypnopompic states and acute stress or anxiety about an ill or endangered relative readily generate a vivid sensed presence or apparition. The percept is a real brain event needing no external signal.
secondaryปัญหาการทดลองThe whole database is retrospective, non-blind, and uncorroborated: unverifiable self-reports, second-hand relays, no independent confirmation of the exact vision time versus the exact death time, and selection so that only striking hits ever reach investigators. No prospective, pre-registered, base-rate-controlled study has ever replicated the effect.
secondaryตีความเกินA real, explicable event — a grief hallucination or a genuine chance coincidence during an emotionally charged period — is overlaid with a supernatural interpretation (telepathy, a departing-soul transmission), reading meaning and mechanism into something already accounted for.
minorข้อมูลรั่วทางปกติOrdinary anticipation mistaken for foreknowledge: the perceiver often already knew the person was gravely ill, elderly, at war, or in danger, so an anxious 'impression' near the time of death reflects rational expectation, not an occult channel.
minorเห็น/ได้ยินผิดOccasionally a real ambiguous stimulus (a shadow, a creak, a half-seen figure) is pattern-completed into the loved one, but most crisis apparitions are full internal hallucinations rather than misperceived external input.
minorคนแกล้งA small residue of embellishment, self-dramatization, or outright hoax exists (as critics of the 1880s SPR casework noted), but deliberate deception is not the main engine of the phenomenon.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
Applies: the claim asserts acquisition of information from beyond ordinary channels. But examine the payload. The 'information' delivered is essentially a single high-prior bit — 'this person is dead/dying now' — for someone often already known to be ill, old, or in danger. That is not un-learnable Layer-2 knowledge (no unlearned language, no un-encountered fact or theorem, no verified un-guessable specific). It is at most a guessable, high-base-rate fact that coincidence alone comfortably supplies. Decisively, genuine Layer-2 has never been delivered: no crisis apparition has, under prospective controlled conditions, conveyed a specific, un-guessable, later-verified detail of the death (exact unusual manner, precise minute, a fact the perceiver could not have inferred). Every strong-looking case is retrospective single-bit matching. So the phenomenon never clears even the threshold at which acquired-from-beyond information would count as evidence.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: Run it prospectively: have people log every crisis impression the moment it occurs — timestamped and sealed/registered before any news arrives — naming the specific person and specific verifiable details (manner and time of death), and count ALL such logs including the misses. Then match against death records with the base rate controlled. Paranormal if timestamped-before-the-news hits carrying un-guessable specific detail massively exceed the false-alarm/base-rate expectation; mundane if hits track the base rate and only retrospectively-dated, detail-free cases survive. In one line: timestamp before the news and count the misses.

confidence: high · Sidgwick, H., Sidgwick, E.M., Johnson, A., Myers, F.W.H., & Podmore, F. (1894). 'Report on the Census of Hallucinations.' Proceedings of the Society for Psychical Research, 10, 25–422; Gurney, E., Myers, F.W.H., & Podmore, F. (1886). Phantasms of the Living. London: Trübner (SPR).
ESTABLISHEDT0Poltergeist / RSPK (objects moving on their own)

อ้าง: A living person under emotional stress (the "focus agent") directly moves, throws, or breaks physical objects by mind alone — recurrent spontaneous psychokinesis — with no physical contact or ordinary force.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantคนแกล้งIn case after case the young 'focus person' is caught producing effects by ordinary means — throwing/kicking objects, knocking, faking voices. The signature tell is that phenomena occur only when no one is watching directly (Enfield's Janet admitted faking part of it; Nickell finds most cases trace to 'an individual motivated to cause mischief,' i.e. a juvenile trickster).
dominantบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงHyperactive agency detection reads intention into random events; confirmation bias makes a household already convinced it is 'haunted' log hits and ignore mundane causes; retrospective matching pins events to whichever person was present, manufacturing a 'focus agent' after the fact. Selective attention makes an unwatched object seem to move 'by itself.'
secondaryความจำแต่งเรื่องWitness reports inflate over retellings: an object that slid or toppled becomes one that 'flew across the room' or 'hovered.' Emotional arousal and group discussion produce shared, embellished, partly false memories that make the final account far more dramatic than the original event.
secondaryเห็น/ได้ยินผิดReal but ordinary stimuli — creaks from a settling house, pipes, drafts, temperature/pressure changes, small vibrations — are misperceived as purposeful movement, and ambiguous sights/sounds are pattern-completed into intentional 'attacks' once fear and expectation prime the observer.
secondaryตีความเกินA genuinely real, fully explainable event (a jug falls, a picture drops, a floor knocks from thermal expansion or nearby traffic/seismic vibration) is real — only the added layer 'a mind/spirit did this' is unsupported. The physics is accepted; the supernatural interpretation is the surplus.
minorสมองสร้างเองStress, fear, and possibly environmental factors (Persinger's contested EM/infrasound and 'sensed presence' work) can generate feelings of dread and presence that make the whole scene feel agentic and threatening, amplifying interpretation — though this explains the felt atmosphere, not object motion.
minorปัญหาการทดลองFor the parapsychology-lab RSPK claims specifically: non-blind, sympathetic investigators documenting a cooperative 'agent,' absent controls, no continuous full coverage, and selective reporting of positive incidents — classic conditions for an artifactual positive result.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้ (ไม่เกี่ยว)
Not an information/knowledge-acquisition claim — it is an object-movement / physical-force claim (a purported ability to move matter, not to obtain un-learnable facts or language). The Layer-1 vs Layer-2 knowledge test therefore does not apply. Note for completeness: even read as an 'ability from beyond' claim, no controlled demonstration of the ability has ever been delivered — RSPK effects reliably cease under complete, blinded observation.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: Prospective test under total, un-interruptible observation: place the marked objects under continuous multi-angle video (including covert cameras) with the focus person unable to touch them, and see whether movement still occurs when no human could physically intervene. Across the literature the effect collapses precisely when observation becomes complete — the hallmark that it is trickery/misperception, not PK.

confidence: high · Houran, J. & Lange, R. (2001), Hauntings and Poltergeists: Multidisciplinary Perspectives (McFarland); Nickell, J. (2012), The Science of Ghosts (Prometheus). RSPK term: Roll & Pratt (1958).

Tier 1 — ข้อมูลปานกลาง

ESTABLISHEDT1Mediumship / spirit channeling (telling you about dead relatives)

อ้าง: A medium receives specific, un-guessable information about a sitter's deceased relatives from the surviving consciousness/spirit of those dead people, i.e. genuine communication across the boundary of death.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงThe reading is a stream of high-base-rate, vague Barnum statements ('a father figure, a chest/heart problem, someone named J, unfinished words left unsaid'). Subjective validation makes the sitter fit these to their own life; confirmation bias and selective attention count the hits and discount the many misses, so a statistically ordinary hit rate feels like specific knowledge. Controlled tests confirm: readings with MORE and MORE GENERAL statements score higher, exactly the opposite of what real information transfer would predict.
secondaryข้อมูลรั่วทางปกติCold reading — the medium fires high-probability guesses, watches the sitter's face, voice, dress, age, reactions, and yes/no replies, then amplifies confirmed guesses and drops missed ones in real time. Any live vocal 'yes/no' leaks age, gender, mood, accent. Where the sitter is known in advance, 'hot reading' (obituaries, Facebook, prior research, confederates) supplies real facts that are then presented as spirit messages.
secondaryความจำแต่งเรื่องAfter the sitting, the sitter reconstructs vague utterances into precise memories ('she KNEW his name was John'), forgets the failed probes, and remembers a cleaned-up, hit-heavy version. Grief and emotional arousal further bias recall toward meaningful matches.
secondaryคนแกล้งSome practitioners deliberately deceive: pre-session research (hot reading), planted confederates, shared 'blue books' of sitter info among mediums, and historically exposed physical mediums (e.g. the Fox sisters' 1888 confession to rapping with their toe joints). Fraud is sufficient to produce dramatic hits but is NOT necessary — honest self-deceived mediums get the same effect via biases above.
minorปัญหาการทดลองFor the lab 'evidence' (e.g. Schwartz's Afterlife Experiments): non-blind or weakly blinded scoring, rater/expectation bias in judging statements as hits, sensory leakage from live sitter responses, non-standardized outcome measures, and undisclosed researcher degrees of freedom. Hyman: results are attributable to 'some combination of fraud, cold reading, rater bias, experimenter error, or chance.'
minorสมองสร้างเองGrief-related spontaneous after-death-communication experiences (sensing/hearing the deceased) are common bereavement phenomena generated by the mourning brain; some channelers use self-induced altered/dissociative states. These supply the felt sense of presence that a reading then exploits, but generate no external information.
minorเห็น/ได้ยินผิดLargely not the driver for verbal readings, but pareidolia/apophenia contributes when sitters read 'signs' (a flickering light, a robin, a coincidental song) as the deceased 'confirming' the reading.
minorตีความเกินA real, explicable event — the genuine emotional catharsis of grieving, plus a few chance-level accurate statements — is overlaid with the supernatural interpretation 'therefore the dead survive and spoke to me.' The comfort is real; the causal story is added on.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
This IS an information-acquisition claim, so the rule bites hard. Layer 1 (allowed/mundane) is all the structure available WITHOUT contact with the dead: population base rates for names/causes-of-death/family roles, and real-time cues leaked by the sitter — a cold reader mines these legitimately. Layer 2 (would be real evidence) is arbitrary, un-learnable, sitter-specific knowledge the medium had no ordinary channel to: the correct unusual name spelled out, a bank PIN, a fact known only to the deceased, the location of hidden property — information that cannot be 'downloaded from nature' or guessed. Genuine mediumship REQUIRES Layer-2 delivery. Layer-2 has never been delivered under controls: double-blind, base-rate-controlled tests return chance results (O'Keeffe & Wiseman 2005), and long-standing prizes for verifiable un-guessable information went unclaimed. Every dramatic 'hit' on inspection reduces to Layer-1 (a common name, a guessed heart condition) plus retrospective matching. No Layer-2, no evidence.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: A prospective, fully double-blind proxy-sitter design: the medium never sees or hears the sitter, produces a reading aimed at one specific deceased target, and the transcript is scored blindly by multiple sitters against the true target AND several decoy targets, with statement specificity and base rates controlled. Genuine spirit contact would let the correct sitter's transcript beat both chance and a cold-reading control group by delivering specific, verified, un-guessable (Layer-2) facts. To date such designs yield chance-level results — the single observation that separates the two readings.

confidence: high · O'Keeffe, C. & Wiseman, R. (2005). Testing alleged mediumship: Methods and results. British Journal of Psychology, 96(2), 165-179 (controlled double-blind test; null result; general statements rated more accurate). Forer, B. R. (1949). The fallacy of personal validation. Journal of Abnormal and Social Psychology, 44, 118-123 (Barnum/subjective-validation effect).
CONTESTEDT1Terminal lucidity (dementia patient suddenly lucid before death)

อ้าง: That a self-aware mind returns intact from a brain physically destroyed by dementia proves consciousness is not produced by the brain — the "true self"/soul was only masked by the diseased tissue and re-emerges as the body dies, evidencing survival independent of neural matter.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantสมองสร้างเองThe episode is a real brain event: transient surges of synchronized gamma activity, altered neurotransmitter dynamics, cortical disinhibition, plus end-of-life factors (steroids, resolving fever/inflammation, metabolic shifts) briefly re-enable already-wired circuits. Borjigin's dying-brain gamma surges (PNAS 2013/2023) are a concrete candidate; Mashour 2019 frames it as network-level return of function, not proof of a non-physical mind.
dominantตีความเกินA genuine, physiologically explicable brief return of function is overlaid with a supernatural reading (the soul re-emerging, mind independent of brain). The real event is not in dispute; the added metaphysics is the unsupported part.
secondaryบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงRetrospective, emotionally charged reporting inflates the signal: dramatic deathbed moments are remembered and retold while non-events are forgotten (selective memory, confirmation). The 60-70% retrospective vs ~6% prospective prevalence gap is a textbook base-rate/recall artifact.
secondaryปัญหาการทดลองThe evidence base is overwhelmingly non-blind retrospective caregiver surveys and anecdote collections with reporting/publication bias; almost no prospective, blinded, neuromonitored data. This inflates both prevalence and the sense of mystery.
minorความจำแต่งเรื่องGrieving family/caregivers reconstruct and embellish how 'lucid' the moment was, upgrading a fragmentary interaction into a full return of personhood (wishful perception noted in the literature as the key thing rigorous studies must exclude).
minorเห็น/ได้ยินผิดA hand-squeeze, eye-opening, a single word, or a smile is over-read as full cognitive/agency return (pareidolia of meaning and intention in ambiguous signals).
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
The paranormal reading treats the returned function as evidence of a mind acquired/preserved from beyond the failing brain, so the info/ability rule applies. But everything ever observed is Layer-1: re-expression of abilities the person already learned and possessed (their own name, native language, recognizing kin, old memories) transiently un-masked by disinhibition/neural surge — no new, un-learnable content. Layer-2 would require the patient to deliver something they could not have learned or known (an unlearned language, a verified un-guessable fact, veridical information with no ordinary channel). Layer-2 has NEVER been documented for terminal lucidity. So the claim delivers only mundane Layer-1 re-activation, not evidence.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: Prospective, blinded observation with continuous EEG/neuromonitoring plus objective cognitive assessment during episodes: if lucidity tracks a measurable physiological brain event (gamma/metabolic surge, steroid or fever change) and its content is limited to the patient's own prior, learned abilities → mundane. The only paranormal-discriminating observation would be a severely demented patient reproducibly producing verified un-learnable Layer-2 information (e.g., fluent output in a never-learned language) with no neural correlate — never observed.

confidence: high · Nahm M, Greyson B, Kelly EW, Haraldsson E (2012), "Terminal lucidity: A review and a case collection," Archives of Gerontology and Geriatrics 55(1):138-142; Mashour GA, Frank L, Batthyany A, et al. (2019), "Paradoxical lucidity: A potential paradigm shift for the neurobiology and treatment of severe dementias," Alzheimer's & Dementia 15(8):1107-1114.
ESTABLISHEDT1Precognitive dreams (dreams that come true)

อ้าง: A dream can deliver specific, un-guessable information about a future event before it happens, meaning the sleeping mind perceives the future directly rather than inferring it.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงThe engine of the phenomenon. Base-rate neglect plus the Law of Truly Large Numbers (Diaconis & Mosteller 1989): a person has hundreds of dream themes per night and billions of dreams occur nightly worldwide, so vague dream-event matches are statistically inevitable. Confirmation bias and selective memory then make the rare 'hit' vivid and memorable while the vast majority of non-matching dreams (and near-misses) are forgotten. The denominator is never counted.
secondaryความจำแต่งเรื่องAfter the event occurs, the fuzzy remembered dream is unconsciously reshaped to fit what happened (hindsight bias, confabulation). The 'match' is manufactured retroactively. Diagnostic proof: when dreams are written down prospectively in a diary before events, the apparent predictive accuracy vanishes.
secondaryตีความเกินA genuinely mundane, explainable event (e.g. dreaming a chronically ill relative dies, then they die) is given a supernatural interpretation. The brain does ordinary implicit forecasting from known information and worries; the resulting plausible-outcome dream is over-read as psychic foresight rather than inference.
minorสมองสร้างเองREM sleep (limbic/visual activation, reduced prefrontal constraint) generates vivid, emotionally salient, loosely-associative narratives spanning a huge range of everyday scenarios — maximizing the raw material that can later be matched to some real event.
minorเห็น/ได้ยินผิดApophenia/pattern-matching: a loose or symbolic resemblance between a vague dream image and a later event is counted as a 'hit,' with the matching criterion applied generously after the fact.
minorปัญหาการทดลองFor the lab strand of precognition claims (e.g. Bem's 'Feeling the Future'), questionable research practices, optional stopping, and publication bias produced small effects that failed to replicate; meta-analytic effect size was indistinguishable from zero.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
The claim is an information-acquisition claim: obtaining future facts from beyond ordinary channels. To count as evidence it would need Layer-2 content — a specific, un-guessable, verifiable fact fixed BEFORE the event and recorded prospectively (e.g. an exact date/name/number that could not be inferred or reshaped). What is actually delivered is at most Layer-1: generic, emotionally salient patterns and plausible extrapolations of daytime concerns, matched loosely and only in hindsight. Layer-2 has never been delivered under prospective, controlled conditions — the accuracy dissolves the moment dreams are logged before the outcome. So the claim requires Layer-2 to be real evidence, and Layer-2 has never appeared.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: Have participants record dreams in timestamped, sealed logs BEFORE any target event, then have independent blind judges match logged dreams against actual events versus a control pool of decoy events. Above-chance matching = paranormal signal; chance-level matching = mundane. Historically the result is at chance, and the retrospective 'hit rate' collapses once recording precedes the event.

confidence: high · Diaconis, P. & Mosteller, F. (1989), "Methods for Studying Coincidences," Journal of the American Statistical Association; Ritchie, S., Wiseman, R. & French, C. (2012), "Failing the Future: Three Unsuccessful Attempts to Replicate Bem's 'Retroactive Facilitation of Recall' Effect," PLoS ONE.
ESTABLISHEDT1Acquired savant (sudden skill after head injury)

อ้าง: A blow to the head "unlocks" latent genius or downloads knowledge/ability the person never possessed, proving a hidden superhuman capacity (or access to a universal mind) that injury merely reveals.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantสมองสร้างเองThis is a genuine brain-state effect. Damage to the left hemisphere (classically left anterior temporal lobe) releases right-hemisphere processing from inter-hemispheric/callosal inhibition ('paradoxical functional facilitation'), with recruitment of undamaged cortex and rewiring. Disinhibition unmasks pre-existing perceptual, computational, and procedural capacity — the ability is real, not imported.
dominantตีความเกินA real, mechanistically explained neurological event gets an added supernatural spin: 'genius from beyond,' 'proof of the soul's hidden powers,' or access to a cosmic consciousness. The paranormal claim lives entirely in this interpretive add-on layered on top of ordinary neuroscience.
secondaryบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงSurvivorship/selective reporting: only the ~90 dramatic transformations get told; the vastly larger base of head injuries producing only deficits is ignored (base-rate neglect). Media and retrospective narratives sharpen the before/after contrast.
minorความจำแต่งเรื่องPost-hoc accounts can exaggerate suddenness and understate prior latent interest, practice, or aptitude, making an unmasked-and-then-honed skill look like an instantaneous gift from nowhere.
minorปัญหาการทดลองThe literature is largely single-case, retrospective, and self-reported; skills are rarely quantified prospectively against a documented pre-injury baseline, leaving room for uncontrolled inflation.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
Layer 1 only. Every documented acquired-savant ability is PATTERN/structure/computation the brain can generate without external instruction: calendar calculation, mathematical/geometric (fractal) pattern, musical harmony and structure, drawing/sculpture, enhanced rote memory. These are disinhibited computational and perceptual capacity — mundane and allowed. Layer 2 (un-learnable arbitrary convention: fluent speech of a never-studied language, or a specific verifiable fact/datum the person could not have encountered) has NEVER been delivered by an acquired savant. Jason Padgett draws fractals but states no unproven theorem; Derek Amato plays music but reports no un-learnable knowledge. No case shows downloaded language or un-encountered facts, so it stays Layer 1 and is not evidence of acquisition from beyond.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: A prospectively documented case in which an acquired savant produces verified Layer-2 content under controlled conditions — e.g., holds a fluent conversation in a language demonstrably never studied, or states a specific, checkable fact they had no ordinary way to know. That would separate 'downloaded knowledge' from 'released computation.' To date only Layer-1 skills appear.

confidence: high · Treffert & Treffert 2021, "The Sudden Savant: A New Form of Extraordinary Abilities," Wisconsin Medical Journal 120(1):46–51; Treffert 2009, "The savant syndrome: an extraordinary condition. A synopsis: past, present, future," Phil. Trans. R. Soc. B 364(1522):1351–1357.
ESTABLISHEDT1Near-death experience (tunnel/light/life-review)

อ้าง: Consciousness detaches from the flatlined brain and perceives a real afterlife realm (and sometimes the resuscitation room from outside the body), proving that mind survives independent of brain function.

สูตรสาเหตุ (cause recipe)
dominantสมองสร้างเองA hypoxic/dying brain generates the whole core script: retinal ischemia plus early-visual-cortex disinhibition produces tunnel-and-light; a gamma-band surge in the temporo-parieto-occipital junction (Borjigin 2013 rats, 2023 dying humans) drives vivid conscious-like imagery and out-of-body feelings; limbic/temporal-lobe activation plus endorphins/NMDA (ketamine-like) and REM intrusion produce euphoria, life-review, and peace. G-force loss of consciousness in fighter pilots reproduces tunnel vision, floating, and dreamlets with no brush with death at all.
dominantตีความเกินA real, increasingly explained brain event is overlaid with a supernatural interpretation. The felt profundity and the cultural/religious frame convert 'my brain did something extraordinary' into 'my soul left my body and saw the afterlife.'
secondaryความจำแต่งเรื่องReports are collected after recovery, not during the event. NDE memories score as MORE detailed and self-referential than real memories (Thonnard 2013), a signature of reconstruction/confabulation; content is elaborated and culturally scripted (Christians meet Jesus, Hindus meet Yamraj) on retelling.
secondaryบังเอิญ+จำแต่ที่ตรงSelective reporting (only survivors talk; the experience is emotionally salient), confirmation and agency detection (interpreting the light/figures as deceased relatives or a divine being), and matching a vague, universal narrative to prior beliefs.
minorข้อมูลรั่วทางปกติCardiac-arrest awareness is not binary; residual or returning consciousness lets patients hear staff and equipment and infer standard resuscitation procedure, later misattributed to disembodied 'seeing' of the room.
minorปัญหาการทดลองVeridical-perception 'hits' come almost entirely from retrospective, non-blind, unverified anecdotes with no timing confirmation; the one prospective control (hidden ceiling targets) removes exactly this artifact and then yields nothing.
minorเห็น/ได้ยินผิดThe tunnel form itself is partly a structural artifact of retinal-ganglion-cell density mapping onto cortex under anoxia (dense center = bright middle, sparse edge = dark surround), i.e. the visual system misrepresenting its own failure as a shape.
กฎ 2 ชั้น · แพทเทิร์น vs ความรู้
APPLIES. The strong claim is an information/ability-acquisition claim: consciousness allegedly obtains real, un-guessable information (the layout of the room seen from the ceiling, or verifiable facts about an afterlife) while the brain is non-functional. That would be Layer-2 knowledge — specific, un-learnable-from-inside-the-body facts. The near-universal CONTENT of NDEs, by contrast, is Layer-1 PATTERN: tunnel, light, peace, floating, life-review — all structures a hypoxic brain can generate internally with no external information required, which is why G-LOC pilots and ketamine users produce the same imagery. Layer-2 has never been delivered: the two large prospective tests (AWARE 2014 and AWARE-II 2023) placed hidden visual targets viewable only from above; across thousands of cardiac arrests, not one NDE/OBE reporter identified a target. Every 'veridical' case that survives is Layer-1-explainable (residual hearing, inference, guessing) or is a retrospective anecdote that fails verification. No un-learnable knowledge = no evidence for survival.

เทสต์ที่แยกจริง/ธรรมดา: Prospective, pre-registered hidden-target detection: place randomized images visible ONLY from a ceiling vantage in resuscitation bays; if patients reporting an out-of-body view reliably report the correct target above chance (timed to the arrest), that is the mundane-vs-paranormal separator. It has been run at scale and returned a null — zero correct identifications.
